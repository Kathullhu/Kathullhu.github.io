Who is David

David is an African American 9th grade student at GEMS World Academy Chicago. He turned 14 on the day this is written, February 3, 2021. He hopes to age quicker. He has three older siblings and is the youngest in his family. He was born in America but both his parents are Ghanaian. Some of the classes he takes include: Mandarin Chinese, Algebra II and Trigonometry, Combined Programming, Physics, Language and Literature, etc. He doesn't know what he wants to do as a carrer path but preferably something in the bio-medical field or computer science.
